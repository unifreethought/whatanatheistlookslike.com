Out Week purpose is to encourage atheists to come out of the closet and own their beliefs publicly. There is no shame in being an atheist, and there are more of us than you think! In fact, if you are a college student, chances are there is a secular group on your campus dedicated to providing a welcoming community for you .

Out Week will run for four days on the University of Northern Iowa campus:

Monday, 9/12:
-Blood drive, Maucker Union Ballroom, 1-5pm
-Casual Coffee Meetup, Maucker Union, 8:30pm
Come hang out with UNIFI, ask any questions you may have, and enjoy some java!


Tuesday, 9/13:
-Dinner with UNIFI, Piazza Private Dining, 5:30pm
Another chance to hear about atheism, UNIFI's community at UNI, and Out Week!


Wednesday, 9/14:
-Movie Night! "Shaun of the Dead"
John Deere Auditorium (CBB 109), 8pm


Thursday, 9/15:
-4th Annual Flying Spaghetti Monster Dinner
Join us in the Commons Ballroom at 6pm for the FSM Dinner! This year the dinner will feature free food (as always), a performance by UNI's Improv Troupe " Half-Masted ", and a charity raffle for the Northeast Iowa Food Bank .
Tickets for the raffle are $1, you can pick one up at our table in the union this Monday-Wednesday from 10-2, and if you happen to see our Pirate Preachers on Thursday, they will have raffle tickets as well. Prizes have been generously donated from businesses in the area.
